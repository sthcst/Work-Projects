  Tip #3 - How to get reservations


    Add textbook badges to attract more students


Dear teacher,

We are sending you these tips in order to help you to get more 
reservations as this is one of the conditions to get the higher Tutor 
Rank in the future. There are various students who want to study 
different lessons, so we would recommend you to request as many badges 
as it is available to have.
*In this way, you will increase the chances to get more regular students 
and Booked lessons with them.*

Native CampNative CampNative CampNative CampNative Camp


      If you don’t have a badge of the lesson which a student is looking
      for at the moment, a student will not be able to book your slots
      or to have lessons with you for the certain topic.

Teacher’s available badges can be found on your public profile page for 
students (example: https://nativecamp.net/waiting/detail/xxxx 
<https://nativecamp.net/waiting/detail/xxxx>, where "xxxx" is your 
Teacher ID)

Teaching material badges are awarded to the teachers who have requested 
them and passed the specified qualifications for badges.

In order to have a good feedback from your students and quality lessons 
please be aware that if you request the badge, you need to get familiar 
with the lesson material and the lesson flow, to be well-prepared before 
you start conducting lessons to your students.
