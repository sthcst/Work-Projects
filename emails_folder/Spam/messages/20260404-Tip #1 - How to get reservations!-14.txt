  Tip #1 - How to get reservations


    Open your slots as much as possible


Dear teacher,

We would like to share some tips on how to have more Booked lessons in 
order to earn more and upgrade your Tutor Rank. One of the conditions to 
upgrade the Tutor Rank is to have a certain number of Booked lessons.


      Make your schedule available!

Native Camp

* This sheet is just an example to show you how it looks like when a 
teacher has most of available slots.


    QUESTION: When can students book your slot?

ANSWER: When you open available slots, students can choose the time to 
book you as their teacher.


    QUESTION: How many slots do you need to open in order to have Booked
    lessons?

ANSWER: We advise you to think about your week schedule in advance and 
to open all slots at your available time in order to get more 
reservations from students. There is no exact number of slots you need 
to open. As more you open slots as more you will have opportunities to 
be booked.


      Cancellation Policy

Please be careful while opening slots because of the cancellation policy 
(do not open slots if you are not sure you will be available in that 
period).
