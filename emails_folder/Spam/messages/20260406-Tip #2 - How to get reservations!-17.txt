  Tip #2 - How to get reservations


    Attract the students with your Tutor's profile


Dear teacher,

In order to upgrade your Tutor Rank, besides opening available slots, 
you also need to attract students to choose you and book your slots. 
Now, we will talk about your Tutor Public Profile. If you have an 
attractive and teasing profile, you will be more interesting for 
students to choose exactly you as their teacher.


      Tips for the self-introduction text:

Use *simple sentences*, so the students can easily understand them.
Write something about your educational background and experience so the 
students can be introduced to *your basic background*.
Write *why do you like this job* and some of your interests.
Use the end of the introduction to write them *what benefits they will 
have by choosing you* for their classes.


      Tips for the introduction video:

*Introduce yourself* at the beginning and say something basic about 
yourself.
Tell the students *why you love this job* and mention your interests.
Mention *the benefits of having you* as their teacher.
Make your own video! *Be unique!*


Students also read the feedback from other students, so try your best to 
be a great teacher, so the students leave you excellent and valuable 
reviews.
